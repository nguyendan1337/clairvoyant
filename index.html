Global & Emerging Markets (CNXT, AFK): Don't overlook opportunities abroad! African markets, represented by AFK, are seeing steady inflows, reflecting optimism in regional economic transformation. Meanwhile, China's innovative ChiNext market (CNXT) is still delivering strong returns despite some recent outflows, highlighting specific pockets of growth in emerging economies.

Industrial & Defense (XAR): With global defense spending on an upward trend, the aerospace and defense sector continues to be a resilient player. XAR has seen substantial inflows, indicating investor confidence in this defensive, yet innovative, industry.

Overall, it looks like investors are strategically balancing high-growth innovation with resilient, defensive plays and opportunistic exposure to global themes. These ETFs offer a diversified way to tap into some of the strongest trends shaping the market right now.
