Markets are navigating mixed signals this month. This environment is fostering a blend of innovation bets and defensive plays.

Innovation & AI: The AI race continues to accelerate, with breakthroughs rapidly transforming semiconductor manufacturing and chip design. Semiconductors: This sector remains pivotal, with AI and machine learning revolutionizing manufacturing processes, driving efficiency and speed. SMH has seen strong 1-year inflows of $661.17M, benefiting from leaders like NVIDIA and TSMC, while XSD has outperformed the S&P 500 over a decade.

Concurrently, South Korea's EWY and FLKR are experiencing a strong rebound, outpacing peers, partly fueled by leading AI chip suppliers like SK Hynix. This highlights a strategic pivot towards both innovation and defensive stability.

How ETFs Were Selected

Raw lists of ETFs were gathered compiled from multiple Yahoo Finance top performance lists. ETFs with leveraged or otherwise risky keywords were filtered out. Only those with a 52-week change above 25%, a 3-month return above 6%, and prices above both their 50-day and 200-day averages were kept. This indicates short, mid, and long term growth and momentum. The list was then sorted by 52 Week Change % to put the absolute best winners at the top. Finally, the list was given to Google Gemini which performed individual searches on those ETFs as well as searches for recent news and real world context to then come up with the final selection of 10-15 picks.
