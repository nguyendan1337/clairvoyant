How ETFs Were Selected

The list of ETFs was compiled from multiple Yahoo Finance lists. ETFs with leveraged or otherwise risky keywords were filtered out. The remaining ETFs were sorted by 52-week change, and only those with a 52-week change above 25%, a 3-month return above 6%, and prices above both their 50-day and 200-day averages were kept.
